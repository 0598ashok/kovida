INTRODUCTION

“Kovida Sahrudaya Foundation” aims to reach out to those in need of social services and offer any assistance we can. Founder Dr. G Anuhya Reddy has taken the initiative with a strong commitment to servicing the needs of underprivileged children, adults, and couples, including underprivileged athletes, emancipated children, and people with special needs. She is a qualified businesswoman with more than 10 years of experience in social work providing high-quality care and support to people who are socially excluded or who are having problems in their lives, who struggle to have further education, who plan to get married but face financial difficulties, and who are talented athletes but are unable to advance in their sport because of their financial struggles. She proved herself to have a demonstrated history of effectiveness in working with people, families, or groups in a range of settings. KSF is renowned for its excellence in organizational and program planning, and has documented success overcoming obstacles like resource scarcity and budgetary limitations to provide high-caliber, cost-effective, and comprehensive service offerings. skilled in creating powerful coalitions, intergovernmental alliances, and charitable support. Dr. G Anuhya Reddy Garu successfully manages the "Kovida Sahrudaya Foundation," a non-profit organization under KOVIDA GROUP (USA, INDIA), KOVIDA GROUP LLC (UAE), with her income, and can vouch that it receives no funding from donors, the general public, or any other sources.

OUR VISION

We envision a society where everyone is treated equally, there is peace and prosperity for all, and education is improved. We imagine a society in which neither a student nor a sportsperson gives up on their endeavours because of a lack of funding. The globe must be celebrating inclusivity, equality, and opportunity.

OUR SERVICES

KSF has never wavered in its commitment to its mission, whether it was promoting hygienic awareness, providing women's sanitary products, masks, and food packets, hosting events and honouring reporters, celebrating the motherhood of 21 women, feeding thousands of people, offering financial support for events or initiatives, or providing essential medical supplies during a global emergency. It supported all the falls and floods by standing like a pillar.
